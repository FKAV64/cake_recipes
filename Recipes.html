Chocolate Cake

This is a classic and simple recipe of a Chocolate cake. Good for children and adults

DifficultyPreparationCooking timeTotal time
Easy10 mins25 mins35 mins

Ingredient

Black chocolate 200g

Sugar 150g

Butter 200g

Egg 4

Flour 80g

Preparation

1

Melt the chocolate with the butter

2

Mix the eggs,sugar and flour

3

Mix everything to form a paste

4

Put in the Oven for 25mins at a temperature 150
